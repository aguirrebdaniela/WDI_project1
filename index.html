Harry's Trivia

Rules of The Game (click here!)

Pick a game mode! In "1 player" mode, you will play against the clock. You have 5 minutes to answer as many questions as possible!
In "2 player" mode, you will play against another player and take turns on the keyboard. Pick a topic and a category (the higher the amount of points on each block, the harder the question). Once all the questions have been answered, the winner will be announced!
Have fun!
